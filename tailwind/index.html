What is Market Basket Analysis?

Market basket analysis is a data mining technique used by retailers to increase sales by better understanding customer purchasing patterns. It involves analyzing large data sets, such as purchase history, to reveal product groupings, as well as products that are likely to be purchased together

How it is Helpful

Market basket analysis can increase sales and customer satisfaction. Using data to determine that products are often purchased together, retail can optimize product placement, offer special deals and create new product bundles to encourage further sales of these combinations.
